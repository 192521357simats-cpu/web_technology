About Gourmet Haven

A dark, polished dining room built around attentive service and thoughtful cuisine.

Since 2018

Crafted for Unhurried Evenings

Gourmet Haven brings together seasonal produce, classic European technique, and a contemporary sense of comfort. Our team designs each menu around balance: richness with freshness, elegance with warmth, and detail without fuss.

From private celebrations to relaxed dinners, every guest is welcomed into an atmosphere shaped by candlelight, gold accents, and quietly confident hospitality.

Executive Chef

Chef Elena Marchetti

Chef Elena leads the kitchen with a focus on seasonal menus, refined sauces, and ingredient-led cooking. Her approach combines formal training with a generous, guest-first spirit.

  • Specializes in modern European cuisine
  • Creates monthly tasting menus
  • Leads private dining and chef table experiences
